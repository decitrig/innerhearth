About Us

Studio owners Jill and Lauren

Inner Hearth Yoga offers alignment-focused hatha yoga classes in our snug studio nestled in the heart of Point Breeze.

Whether you’re looking for an asana challenge or total rest and relaxation, we’ve got a class that will fit your needs. No matter what class you choose, you’ll get individual attention from our skilled and attentive teachers to help you deepen and focus your practice.

What class should I take?

If you want to Stretch and Strengthen, try:

  • Aligned Flow Level 1 (beginner/intermediate)
  • Aligned Flow Level 2 (intermediate/advanced)
  • Yoga for Runners and Athletes

If you want to Rest and Restore, try:

  • Yin Yoga
  • Yin Yoga & Flow
  • Meditation
  • Yin Yogassage

For expecting mothers and new mothers:

  • Prenatal and Postnatal Yoga
  • Baby and Me Yoga

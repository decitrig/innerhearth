Making Up Missed Classes

Going to a weekly class is great - it helps make yoga practice a regular part of your life - but sometimes you need to miss a class or two. When this happens, you have until the end of the session to MAKE UP your missed classes! If you know you’re going to miss class in advance, you can even make up BEFORE you miss class.

Many people enjoy taking class on alternate days of the week - for example, register for one class a week and attend class on alternate Tuesdays and Thursdays.

How to make the most of your session at Inner Hearth

To DROP IN to one class at at time:

  1. Click the “Home” button on the left
  2. Click "Log In" on the bottom left
  3. Click on the name of the class you want to take
  4. Scroll down until you see the words "Single Day Registration" and click on the calender date when you want to take class
  5. Click "Register for One Day"

To sign up for a class you want to attend EVERY WEEK:

  1. Click the “Home” button on the left
  2. Click "Log In" on the bottom left
  3. Click on the name of the class you want to take
  4. Click "Register for the entire session"

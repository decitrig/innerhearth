Foundations of Flow

Sunday, January 26 from 1-3pm and Thursday, February 6 from 7-9pm with Maggie Ryan

Vinyasa means "to place in a special way." In this workshop, we will bring more awareness to each pose within a Sun Salutation. Join us as we break down the mechanics of downward facing dog, upward facing dog, low cobra, and the infamous chaturanga dandasana. We will build strength and clarity in each of these poses, and learn how to smoothly and safely link them together. All levels welcome!

3 Weeks to Handstand!

Sundays, Febrary 9, 16 and 23 from 11am-12:30pm with Lauren

Can you rock half-handstand at the wall no problem, but feel befuddled when you think about kicking up to full handstand? This is the class for you. We’ll break down the mechanics of standing on your hands, and practice exercises to help you overcome the special challenges of defying gravity. At the same time, you’ll grow stronger and more confident from focusing on this pose over time! You’ll finish this workshop with the understanding to confidently practice Handstand during the course of a normal yoga class. Open to students who can go into half-handstand at the wall.

Rest for the Weary: A Restorative Yoga Benefit Workshop

For the Women's Shelter of Greater Pittsburgh

Friday, December 20 from 5:30-7:30pm, with Lauren

Take time this holiday season to rest, and in doing so, lend a helping hand to members of our community who are in upheaval during this time. 100% of the proceeds from this workshop will go to the Adopt a Family program at the Women's Shelter, which helps women and their families to maintain a sense of self and autonomy while they're transitioning out of harmful situations. In return, you'll be treated to two blissful hours of candle-lit restoratives, reminding you that no matter your situation, a rested body is a gift beyond price!

Winter Solstice Practice

Saturday, December 21 from 3-5pm with Jill & Lauren

On the shortest day of the year, take refuge at Inner Hearth to pause and reflect. Our practice, from 3-5pm, will start in the light and end in the dark as we welcome the contemplative time of winter and set an intention for the new year to come.

All are welcome at this practice! We'll start with several rounds of sun salutations to acknowledge the setting sun, and continue with soothing restorative and Yin Yoga poses as the evening settles in. We'll close with a long meditation to seal our intentions for the return of the sun.

All Levels Welcome!

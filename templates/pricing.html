Why Sessions?

Inner Hearth Yoga offers classes in sessions, like semesters! Just like a class offered at a university, each weekly class builds on previous classes. This progressive teaching style helps students grow measurably stronger and more flexible throughout the session.

Committing to a session not only builds your practice but builds a strong, supportive yoga community. Making a commitment to your practice means making a commitment to you!

Session pricing

When you register for the session after it begins, we'll pro-rate your pricing according to how many weeks are left in the session. If you miss your registered class, you may make up that class in ANY other class on our schedule! You can make up in advance or after you miss, and you’ve got until the end of the session to take your makeup classes. (You may not make up a missed class in Yin Yogassage.)

Make a wedding party, baby shower or retreat memorable with a partner yoga workshop! During this two-hour workshop led by Jill and Lauren, every pose is done with the support of a friend. Expect to do plenty of laughing and falling over - who says yoga isn’t a full-contact sport? No previous yoga experience is necessary - and this workshop is for partners of all kinds. Eight person minimum. Email info@innerhearthyoga.com for rates and scheduling.

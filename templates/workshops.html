Restorative Yoga for Self Care

Thursday, August 1 from 7-9pm, with Lauren

Restorative yoga poses are done with the full support of of props like blankets, bolsters, blocks, straps, or simply the nearest wall. Add a deep, steady breath and the intention to release tension into the mix, and you've got a powerful two hours designed to nurture and refresh you no matter how busy you may be.

During this workshop, we’ll begin with an exploration of self-care techniques like tennis ball massage and a few simple stretches to open up and prepare the body that you can do at work, home, or wherever you are. Then we’ll dive into a deeply nourishing restorative practice with some guided meditations offered during the poses to make it easier to settle your mind as you settle into your body!

All are welcome, special conditions will be accommodated. Email Lauren with questions.
